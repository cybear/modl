Welcome to the Malmö Open Device Lab

Malmö Open Device Lab or simply modl is an effort to provide the Malmö web developer community access to a wide range of mobile device to test their work.

In today's constantly evolving device landscape it is quite difficult for most organizations to create a device pool diverse enough to cover even the more common brand / model / os combinations. Of course, any testing is better than no testing, but for a website specifically aimed at mobile devices, that may be insufficient. This is where we would like to help.

We are working to collect devices that many of us have in the back of drawers, hidden away, forgotten. If you are no longer using an old device, let other take advantage of it and use it as a test device. It will naturally remain yours, you can take it back at any time. You are just lending it to a group of grateful individuals.
